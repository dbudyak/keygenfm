Keygen FM is a 24/7 internet radio station dedicated to chiptune, cracktro, and demoscene music. Experience the nostalgic sounds of vintage computer music with a retro Windows 95-styled interface.

<b>Features:</b>
• Stream high-quality MP3 audio 24/7
• Real-time now playing information
• Album artwork display
• Background playback support
• Persistent media notification with playback controls
• Volume control
• Live listener count
• Classic Windows 95 aesthetic with pixel-perfect design

The app connects to a self-hosted AzuraCast radio server, providing reliable streaming and up-to-date track metadata. Perfect for fans of chiptune, keygen music, MOD/XM/IT tracker formats, and classic demoscene productions.

No ads. No tracking. Just pure chiptune bliss.
